Lemon Salt Roasted Potatoes

In this recipe for Lemon Salt Roasted Potatoes, fresh herbs are blended into lemony kosher salt provide amazing flavor. I want you to take a good, close look at that photo above. These lemon salt roasted potatoes turn out crisp and wonderful on the outside, and pillowy soft on the inside. They’re salty, lemony and buttery, and the fresh herbs added in make them perfect. This is the next side dish you should make for dinner.


Ingredients needed:

  1. fresh sage
  2. fresh rosemary
  3. fresh thyme
  4. finely grated lemon zest
  5. kosher salt
  6. small potatoes
  7. extra virgin olive oil
  8. unsalted butter
  9. freshly ground black pepper

How to make Lemon Salt Roasted Potatoes:

he full, printable recipe is at the end of this post. Preheat the oven to 425°F. Spray two rimmed baking sheets with nonstick spray or line with parchment paper. In a small bowl, mix the sage, rosemary, thyme, lemon zest and salt. Use a wooden spoon to press the herbs into the salt mixture. Keep pressing and mixing until flavors are melded together. (You can do this in a mini-food processor if you have one). In a large bowl, toss the potatoes with the oil and butter and season with pepper. Spread the potatoes in a single layer on the prepared baking sheets. Roast for 25 minutes.
